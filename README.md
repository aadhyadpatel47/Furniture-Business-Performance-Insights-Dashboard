# Furniture Business Performance Insights Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard that gives furniture retailers a unified view of sales performance, inventory health, pricing strategy, and product demand — built to support data-driven retail decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objective

Furniture businesses managing large inventories rarely have one place to see what's selling, what's sitting on shelves, and how discounts are cutting into margins.

This dashboard consolidates revenue, inventory, pricing, and demand into a single decision-making surface.

---

## 🛠 Tech Stack

| Tool | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Power BI Desktop | Data visualization & reporting |
| Power Query | Data cleaning & transformation |
| DAX | Business metrics & KPI calculation |
| Data Modeling | Category & product relationship mapping |
| File Format | `.pbix` |

---

## 📂 Data Source

Retail furniture sales & inventory dataset covering multiple categories and time periods:

- Product categories & individual SKUs
- Revenue, order volume & average selling price (ASP)
- Discount rates & revenue impact
- Inventory levels & turnover
- Time-based sales trends
